The Porsche Carrera GT (Project Code 980)

The Porsche Carrera GT is a marvel of automotive engineering. With a V10 engine, carbon fiber construction, and iconic design, it's a symphony of power and elegance.

Limited Production

Porsche only produced the Carrera GT in limited quantities. The production run of this supercar was between 2004 and 2006, with only 1,270 units produced. This limited production not only adds to its exclusivity but also makes it one of the rarer and more collectible supercars in the world.

Legendary Porsche Carrera GT: Supercar Icon of Unrivaled Performance and Design.

The Porsche Carrera GT boasts a top speed of 205 mph, a 5.7-liter V10 engine generating 612 horsepower, and can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.5 seconds.
